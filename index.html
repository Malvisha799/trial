How Does India Certify Help You?

The National Single Window System (NSWS) is a digital platform to guide you in identifying and applying for approvals according to your business requirements. The Know Your Approvals (KYA) module includes guidance for 32 Central Departments and 34 States. You are advised to check the relevant Government portals for any other required approvals. The portal hosts applications for approvals from 32 Central Departments and 28 State Governments. These approvals can be applied through NSWS.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with certification?

To begin your certification process, create an account and select your desired certification program. Our team will guide you through the requirements and next steps.

What documents are required?

Required documents typically include government ID proof, educational certificates, and relevant work experience documentation. Specific requirements vary by certification type.

How long does the certification process take?

The certification timeline varies depending on the type of certification and your preparation. Most certifications can be completed within 2-4 weeks after document submission.
